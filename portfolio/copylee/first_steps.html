Payments Platform

Powerful and easy one-off payments.

Start accepting payments instantly with Plasso. Setting up product pages, called 'Spaces', to sell your digital or physical goods/services is super easy. You can sell multiple products per Space and even collect custom data from customers at checkout.

Customer Management

Know your customers.

Anytime someone purchases your products or you receive a payment you'll be able to manage those payments and customers from within Plasso. You can issue refunds, view transaction history and even edit/update your customer data.

Flexible Payments

Take pre-orders and accept donations.

Capture pre-orders to charge your cusotmer's credit cards at a later time. Your customers can manage their own pre-order and even keep their credit card up to date.
Accepting donations is easy; with one click all the language on the payment form will be donation specific.

Sell Anything

Shipping support and digital file delivery.

Collect shipping addresses at checkout and send shipment notifications to your customers with one click. Sell your digital goods and files: ebooks, videos, music, etc. Plasso takes care of the secure file delivery.
