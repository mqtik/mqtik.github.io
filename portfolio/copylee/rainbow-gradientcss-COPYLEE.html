About Us

Dreams demand realization. Ideas demand execution. That premise is the motivation behind Glitch. Started as an urban art-gallery in the heart of Jakarta, we set to become patron to artists around the globe by developing their ideas into tangible forms such as toys, comics and many other.

We are on to stir the entertainment industry. To package and present the beautiful randomness into a cohesive medium to the masses and to give color to the mundane.

- whether they are in the form of sculptures, figurines, paintings, prints, books or graphic novels. Each with their own story, each with their own soul -
